Here is my first paragraph. What sort of content will you find on this site? Keep reading to find out!

This site is a place to share some of my analog photography, mainly on 35mm film and with a very
small amount of medium format (120) film. I started taking photos with a hand-me-down 35mm film
camera from my dad in 2009. Since then I've had a lot of fun learning about different cameras and
film types, and even ventured into developing my own film in my bathtub!
